Hylian Tomato Pizza

Well met, adventurer! Ever wanted to eat the pizza your favorite Hylian hero eats? Well look to further!

A pizza made with fresh Hylian tomato. SLices of melty Hateno cheese make it irrestible

Picture it now. Your princess is yet again in another castle and in the grasp of calamity gannon nonetheless. But before you embark on your journey of smashing pots cutting grass you need to fill that bottomless stomach of yours. Follow this recipe and cure thine famish.

Ingredients

  • Tabantha Wheat
  • Hylian Tomato
  • Hateno Cheese

Steps

  1. Combine Tabantha Wheat, Hylian Tomato, and Hateno Cheese into a pot
  2. Cook for 5 seconds
  3. Enjoy!
